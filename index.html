T&T Resort Phu Quoc

Where Sunset Meets Wellness & Adventure

Executive Summary

T&T Resort, located on beautiful Phu Quoc Island, combines leisure, wellness, and adventure in a boutique lifestyle destination. Our vision includes a rooftop cocktail bar with live sunset music, a wellness-focused spa, health shop, restaurant, and an integrated tour company — TNT Tours. The resort targets the growing market for immersive, wellness, and experiential travel.

Business Model Components

1. Rooftop Cocktail Bar (5 PM–11 PM daily) featuring themed cocktails, snacks, and live music during sunset.

2. Wellness Zone with spa treatments, a health-focused restaurant, and a wellness shop.

3. TNT Tours offering curated island tours, BBQ boat charters, and private experiences booked via the resort.

Staffing & Compensation

Initially staffed with 3 full-time employees:

  • Manager/Receptionist (handling bookings and front desk)
  • Bar/Kitchen Lead
  • Spa Lead

Staff are paid 6–8 million VND/month plus 5–10% commission on sales.

Volunteers supplement operations, receiving accommodation, meals, and commission-based incentives.

Additional Opportunities & Recommendations

  • Volunteer program for cost savings and community engagement.
  • Corporate wellness packages: team-building, yoga, detox retreats.
  • Seasonal/off-peak specials to boost low season revenue.
  • Retail expansion via online wellness product sales.
  • Sustainability initiatives like solar panels, rainwater harvesting.
  • Loyalty programs encouraging repeat customers.
  • Partnerships with local businesses for cross-promotion.
